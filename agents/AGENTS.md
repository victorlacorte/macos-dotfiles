## General Guidelines

- Never use em dash (`—`, U+2014). Instead, use the plain dash (`-`, U+002D). When any dash would normally be used as punctuation, prefer rewriting the sentence with commas, parentheses, or a colon if that results in more natural prose.
- When making technical decisions, do not give much weight to development cost. Instead, prefer quality, simplicity, robustness, scalability, and long term maintainability.
